Going abroad

Study Abroad


Students who wish to do so may study for one semester during their second year of DUT or during their year of professional license.
For this, students must apply during the year preceding their departure.

More information on terms

The partner establishments of the IUT:

University of Quebec at Chicoutimi

UQAC welcomes students for one semester, replacing a semester completed at the IUT.

  • Audience: students from all IUT departments in DUT2 - semester 4
  • number of students: 3 places, possibility of additional places depending on the year
  • language of instruction: French
  • mobility period: early January to late April

See the UQAC website

Conference of Rectors and Principals of Universities of Quebec

CREPUQ brings together the Universities of Quebec, mainly French-speaking, but also an English-speaking university.

  • Audience: students from all departments in DUT 2 - semester 4
  • number of students: unlimited
  • language of instruction: French or English
  • mobility period: early January to late April
Voir le site du la CREPUQ

Institute of Technology Sligo (Ireland) - program Erasmus +

The Sligo Technological Institute is located in the north-west of Ireland.

  • Public: students of the professional license in the Tourism professions: Marketing of tourism products - Marketing of Tourist, Accommodation, Cultural and Sports Infrastructures.
  • number of students: 3 students
  • language of instruction: English level B2
  • mobility period: early January to late April

Institute of Technology Sligo website

Dundalk Institute of Technology - Erasmus + program

The Dundalk Technology Institute is located in the north east of Ireland.

  • Audience: students of DUT Informatique or MMI and of the professional license in Tourism Professions: Marketing of tourism products - Marketing of Tourist, Accommodation, Cultural and Sports Infrastructures.
  • number of students: 1 student (INFO & MMI) and 1 student from LP CITHECS
  • language of instruction: English level B2
  • mobility period: early January to late April

Dundalk Institute of Technology website

Pablo Olavide University - Seville (Spain) - Erasmus + program

  • Audience: DUT TC and GEA
  • number of students: 2 students
  • language of instruction: Spanish level B1
  • mobility period: early January to late April

Website Pablo Olavide University - Seville


Internships abroad


Students can complete their 2 th year or professional license internship as a graduation project in a university laboratory of a partner institution. In this context:

  • DUT 1 students apply in June for an internship which takes place in the 2nd th year.
  • Professional License students apply in September for their end-of-year internship.

Students also have the opportunity to find an internship abroad on their own.

Who can benefit from an experience abroad?


Audience: first-year students as part of a short-term internship. It is necessary to find your internship yourself.

Second year students of DUT or professional license, when they are not doing a work-study program, can benefit from the contacts of the IUT

These students can also find an internship abroad on their own.



How to apply?


Regular information meetings allow students to keep informed of the steps to be taken, which may vary from country to country.

The IUT of Lens helps and accompanies students in the construction of their application and in the preparation of their stay.

Submit an application

Financial aid


Various financial aids exist to help students achieve their mobility for studies or internships. The eligibility conditions and the amounts vary depending on the funding.

More information on funding and how to apply to the International Relations Department of the University of Artois.
