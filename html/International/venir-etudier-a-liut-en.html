Come study at IUT

French skills.

All IUT courses are taught in French. It is therefore necessary for the student to understand and express himself fluently in the French language. For DUT and professional licenses, level B2 is required. Anyone who does not have French nationality can decide to validate their skills in the French language through different diplomas and tests.

The Ministry of National Education thus offers the DELF (Diploma in French Language Studies) divided into four sub-categories:

  • DELF A1: the promotion of first achievements
  • DELF A2: level of "survival", elementary competence
  • DELF B1: the threshold level
  • DELF B2: the independent user
